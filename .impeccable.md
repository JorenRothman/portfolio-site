## Design Context

### Users
Developers seeking inspiration or collaboration. They appreciate craft, attention to detail, and distinctive design choices.

### Brand Personality
Minimal & Refined — Clean, sophisticated, editorial. But with bold accents drawn from strong portfolio references. Think: refined typography, generous whitespace, and confident statements.

### Aesthetic Direction
- Clean editorial layout with bold typographic moments
- Strategic motion using GSAP (already installed)
- Refined color palette with a single bold accent
- Generous spacing as a design element
- Full-bleed hero moment in the header

### Design Principles
1. **Typography as hero** — Use distinctive display fonts for headlines, creating dramatic scale contrasts
2. **Space is design** — Generous whitespace (100-200px gaps) creates luxury and confidence
3. **Motion with purpose** — Staggered entrances, smooth ease-out-quart transitions, scroll-triggered reveals
4. **Bold accent strategy** — One bold color (e.g., electric blue or vivid orange) that pops against monochrome
5. **Purposeful asymmetry** — Escape centered layouts for tension-filled compositions
