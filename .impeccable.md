## Design Context

### Users

Visitors to a personal developer portfolio - potential clients, employers, collaborators, or fellow developers. They land on the site to learn about skills, experience, and projects. Context: casual discovery or deliberate recruitment/networking.

### Brand Personality

**Developer with edge** - A full-stack developer who values clean code AND distinctive design. Not a generic template portfolio, but a memorable statement piece that reflects technical competence through its execution.

Three words: **Confident, Distinctive, Technical**

### Aesthetic Direction

**Brutalist/Raw** - Bold typography that dominates the viewport, exposed structural elements, high contrast with intentional "raw" aesthetic. Thick borders, offset shadows, geometric decorative elements. The design should feel like a statement, not a template.

Anti-references (what this should NOT look like):

- Generic blue/purple gradient portfolios
- Glassmorphism cards
- Neon-on-dark "hacker" aesthetics
- Centered, perfectly balanced safe layouts
- Rounded corners everywhere

### Design Principles

1. **Typography as architecture** - Text should feel structural, not decorative. Oversized, tight tracking, black weights.
2. **Contrast creates hierarchy** - Make important things 5-10x larger than supporting elements. Nothing medium-sized.
3. **Raw is intentional** - Thick borders, offset shadows, geometric shapes aren't accidents - they're features.
4. **Warmth over cool** - Use amber/orange accents instead of cold blue. Brutalism can still feel human.
5. **Motion with purpose** - Animations should feel aggressive and confident, not bouncy or playful.

### Color Palette

- **Accent**: Warm amber (oklch 70% 0.18 60) - energetic, distinctive from typical tech blue
- **Background Light**: Warm cream (oklch 98% 0.01 60)
- **Background Dark**: Near-black warm (oklch 10% 0.02 60)
- **Text**: Near-black warm (oklch 12% 0.02 60) / Light cream (oklch 95% 0.01 60)

### Typography

- **Display**: Syne - bold, geometric, distinctive
- **Body**: Outfit - clean, modern, readable
- Hero text: Up to 11rem (mobile) / aggressive tracking
- Section titles: Up to 9xl with accent underlines
